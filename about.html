About Jobs

We're on a mission to build technology that empowers people and transforms industries. Join us in shaping the future.

Our Story

Jobs started in a small garage in Mombasa with a simple idea: technology should empower people, not complicate their lives. What began as a passion project between two friends has grown into a global company serving millions of users.

Today, we're a team of over 500 talented individuals across multiple continents, but we've never lost sight of our original mission. We remain committed to building products that are intuitive, powerful, and accessible to everyone.

Our culture is built on innovation, collaboration, and a genuine care for our team members. We believe that happy, fulfilled employees create exceptional products.
