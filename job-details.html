Senior Frontend Engineer

Nairobi Full-time Posted 2 days ago
Apply Now

About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Frontend Engineer to join our growing engineering team. You will work on building and scaling our web applications, collaborating with designers and backend engineers to create seamless user experiences.

What You'll Do

  • Design and implement new features for our web applications
  • Collaborate with designers to translate mockups into pixel-perfect interfaces
  • Optimize application performance and ensure cross-browser compatibility
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to code reviews
  • Participate in technical planning and architecture decisions

What We're Looking For

  • 5+ years of experience with React, TypeScript, and modern frontend technologies
  • Strong understanding of web performance optimization and accessibility
  • Experience with state management libraries (Redux, MobX, or similar)
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and equity
  • Comprehensive health benefits
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Professional development budget
  • Generous PTO and parental leave
  • Modern office and equipment
